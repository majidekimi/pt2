Procedure for Making a Toasted Peanut Butter Sandwich

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.

  2. Toast the bread slices in a toaster or on a skillet until golden brown and crispy.

  3. Remove the toasted bread from the toaster or skillet.

  4. Spread peanut butter evenly on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread.

  5. If desired, spread jelly or jam on the unbuttered side of the other slice of bread.

  6. Put the two slices of bread together, with the peanut butter and jelly sides facing each other.

    7. Enjoy your toasted peanut butter sandwich!
